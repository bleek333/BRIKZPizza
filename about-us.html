About Us

BRIKZ was founded in Davis, California in 2018. The BRIKZ food truck hit the road in 2020 after the COVID-19 pandemic hit. Since transitioning to the food truck life BRIKZ hasgreatly enjoyed its loyal followers, and new customers. You can find the BRIKZ trucks and team at your local breweries, wineries, and local events weekly. To find a detailed schedule of where we will be next follow our instagram and twitter.
